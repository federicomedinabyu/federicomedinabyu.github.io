Description of Target

The main target is the people who live in the surroundings and have the ability to use a computer and the internet. The special focus is that people are informed about the weather, but we also want to include a lot of information about the different activities that take place in the city as well as local news and personal publications.

First Persona

Ezequiel Gonzalez, He is 60 years old and works in the fields every day. His passion is watching his crops grow. His family of 5 helps him cultivate and protect the crop. For him it is essential to be informed about the weather to avoid frost and be prepared for droughts.

Second Persona

Maria Gimenez has just arrived in the city after her divorce, she has three children and needs to find a place to rent and a school to send her children. Local advertisements on the site can help her find what she is looking for.

Scenarios

New people have come to town and are looking to enroll their children in school. There are also people who are looking for a place to rent. This site will be the best place for them to find what they are looking for

The site is very interactive and is constantly updated with detailed information on the weather.

It is important to be informed when the snow begins to fall. A cautious person prepares in the best way and must take all precautions to go out on the road.

Community announcements are important because sometimes we tend to forget important dates. So we can see on the page what is happening in the community.

This year the community has suffered some incidents of insecurity. Photos of suspicious people have been posted on the site so that neighbors are vigilant and thus can prevent theft.

People who have fields and work the land, must have updated information to know if they will have abundant water for their crops.
